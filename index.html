ولاية الحمراء

Al Hamra, located in the Ad Dakhiliyah Governorate of the Sultanate of Oman, is known for its beautiful heritage and ancient mud-brick buildings. It boasts stunning mountain villages such as Misfat Al Abriyeen, which attracts visitors with its breathtaking scenery and traditional houses. The region is also renowned for its falaj irrigation system, agriculture, and handicrafts, and is considered one of the few areas that have preserved Omani traditions and ancient customs.

ولاية نزوى

Nizwa, located in the Ad Dakhiliyah Governorate of the Sultanate of Oman, is one of the oldest and most famous wilayats (provinces) in the country. It is renowned for its historic fort and traditional souk (market), and has been a center of learning and religion throughout the ages. Its natural beauty, mountains, and oases make it a distinctive tourist and cultural destination.
